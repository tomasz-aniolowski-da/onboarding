## Timesheets — Clockify

- URL: https://app.clockify.me
- Accept the Clockify invite from your email and log in
- Navigate to **Timesheet** tab (top left)
- Click **Select project** and search for your assigned project
- Log your hours daily or weekly — exact hours, as they are billed to the client
- Format: `8:00` (hh:mm)
- For leave or holidays, select the appropriate project (Annual leave, Public holiday, etc.) and enter hours

## Invoicing

- At the end of each month, submit your invoice to: **invoices@datavid.com**
- An invoice template should be provided to you — ask if you haven't received one

Contact: **Sumit Handa** — Sumit.Handa@datavid.com (finance / timesheets)

## Client Account

After Datavid onboarding, you will need to wait for your client account to be set up. You will receive an email — follow the instructions to set your password and MFA. Until then, use this time to familiarize yourself with internal tools and processes.
